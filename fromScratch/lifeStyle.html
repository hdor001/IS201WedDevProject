A Common Misunderstanding

All too often, health is associated with fad diets. As the number of people dying from over-eating compared to under-eating goes up, it is important to remember to avoid the extremes such as bingeing and self-induced starvation. These are not healthy coping mechanisms. The ultimate goal is to find BALANCE.
